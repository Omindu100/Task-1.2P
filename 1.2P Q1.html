Popular Sports

Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in many parts of the world, is the world's most popular sport. It's played and watched by millions of fans globally.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport known for its high-flying dunks and competitive nature. It's a favorite sport in the United States and beyond.

Tennis

Tennis is an individual or doubles sport played on a rectangular court. It's famous for major tournaments like Wimbledon and the US Open.

Golf

Golf is a precision sport played on lush green courses. It's enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.

Swimming

Swimming is a popular aquatic sport and a vital life skill. It offers a range of health benefits and competitive opportunities.

Baseball

Baseball is America's pastime, known for its rich history, iconic stadiums, and passionate fan base.
